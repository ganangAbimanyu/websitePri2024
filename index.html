About PRI



legends of PRI

Pesta Rakyat Indonesia, a grand annual cultural festival, PRI 2024's theme is "JANALOKA LEGENDA," brings the enchanting world of Indonesia's legends and myths to life. With a rich heritage steeped in folklore, this event transports attendees into the heart of Indonesia’s timeless tales, where mythical heroes, and legendary kingdoms unfold in a vivid display of art, culture, and music!

Pesta Rakyat Indonesia is not just a celebration of the past—it’s a bridge to the future, inspiring pride in Indonesia’s diverse folklore and culture. With its Legends and Myths theme, this year’s festival promises to captivate audiences with a magical blend of tradition, art, and music, while fostering unity and cultural appreciation among all who attend.
